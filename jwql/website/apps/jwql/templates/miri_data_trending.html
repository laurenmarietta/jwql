MIRI Data Trending Monitor

How it works

The MIRI data trending dashboard is prepared for instrument engineers to keep tracking the trend of relevant parameters. A back-end software processes a daily chunk of data and calculates averages and deviations of all engineering values that correspond to specific mnemonic identifiers. The outcome is stored in a seperate database and shown on the dashboard below. Since a cron job populates the database over time, the app allows to keep track of the general value trend caused by degadiation and radiation.
